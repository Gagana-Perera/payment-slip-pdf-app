Put your two header images here with these names:
- public/images/header-logo.png (or .jpg / .jpeg)
- public/images/header-bg.png (or .jpg / .jpeg)

How they are used in the PDF:
- header-logo: left side logo panel
- header-bg: right side main banner background

Backward compatibility:
- Older names still supported: header-banner-1 / header-banner-2 / header-banner.
